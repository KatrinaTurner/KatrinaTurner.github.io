Predicting Student Success

A final project for my Machine Learning and Data Science. I used grades and various background info on students to create a model that predicts whether a student would be successful in Algebra 2.

Overview

This was my final project for my Machine Learning and Data Science classes. I used grades and various background info on students to create a model that predicts whether a student would be successful in Algebra 2. The data came from the high school I used to teach at and I gave my model back to the math department for them to use with future students.
